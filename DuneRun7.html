Join the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Dune Run 7

A Celebration of Namibia’s Iconic Dune 7

On July 12th, get ready for an adventure like no other at the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Dune Run 7. This event is more than just a race; it’s a celebration of Namibia’s famous Dune 7, the tallest dune in the coastal desert and a true symbol of endurance.

Dune 7 stands at an impressive 383 meters, inspiring explorers and locals for generations. Now, it’s your turn to take on this majestic giant! The Dune Run 7 invites runners from Namibia and around the world to experience the thrill of running through Namibia’s challenging desert landscape.

This is your chance to connect with Namibia’s heritage, push your personal limits, and honor the strength and beauty of Dune 7. It’s an unforgettable opportunity to celebrate resilience, unity, and the incredible spirit of adventure that defines Namibia’s stunning desert.

Event Details:

  • Date: July 12th
  • Start Location: Dune 7, Coastal Desert
  • Categories: 2km, 5km, 10km, 21km
  • Entry Fees:
    • 2km – N$80
    • 5km, 10km, and 21km – N$150

As a special thank you to all participants, free merchandise will be available to take home.

This is a race for adventurers, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone who’s ready to push their limits while celebrating the natural beauty and strength of Dune 7. Register now and be part of the ultimate desert experience that celebrates endurance, community, and the spirit of Namibia.
