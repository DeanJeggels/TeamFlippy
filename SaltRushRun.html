Join the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Salt Rush Run

A Celebration of Fitness, Nature, and Community

On August 9th, come experience the Salt Rush Run—an event that’s more than just a race. It’s an exciting opportunity to enjoy Namibia’s stunning salt flats and coastline, all while bringing people together to celebrate health, fitness, and the natural beauty of Swakopmund.

Whether you're a beginner, a seasoned runner, or someone who simply loves the outdoors, the Salt Rush Run is the perfect event for you. The run takes you along a breathtaking coastal route, offering amazing views of the salt flats and the stunning coastline that make Namibia unique. As you race alongside fellow participants, you’ll not only experience the thrill of the run but also the joy of being part of a community that cares about the environment.

By joining the Salt Rush Run, you’re also supporting our goal to protect Namibia’s coastline and keep our environment clean and beautiful for future generations.

Event Details:

  • Date: August 9th
  • Start Location: Beginning of the Salt Pans, Swakopmund
  • Categories: 2km, 5km, 10km, 21km
  • Entry Fees:
    • 2km – N$80
    • 5km, 10km, and 21km – N$150

As a thank you for your participation, free merchandise will be given to all runners.

This is your chance to push yourself, meet new people, and be part of something special that not only benefits you but also helps protect Namibia’s incredible natural world. Register now and join us for a day of fun, fitness, and community spirit at the Salt Rush Run!
