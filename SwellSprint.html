Push Beyond Limits at the Enduro Sprint!

Are you ready to redefine your boundaries? Join us for the Enduro Sprint on Saturday, 4th October, starting at the iconic Mole in Swakopmund. This high-energy event offers two exciting race options: Swim and Run or Cycle and Run. With distances for every challenge seeker, it’s your time to push beyond limits and show what you’re made of!

Why You Should Join:

  • Experience the perfect mix of water, wheels, and running in Namibia’s stunning coastal setting.
  • Compete in a supportive and electric atmosphere alongside fellow athletes.
  • Celebrate your achievement with family and friends at the Mole.
