Mole to Mall swim

Get ready for an exciting adventure at Teamflippy’s Mole to Mall Swim Challenge on March 31st, 2024, a Sunday you won't forget! The action kicks off at Waschmachine beach near the Mole rocks at 09:00 am, but don't forget to register starting at 8:20 am. Picture this: a refreshing 3-kilometer swim guided by boats and kayaks, starting at Washmachine Beach and ending at Platz am Meer's beautiful boat launch area. The challenge begins at 9 am and wraps up by 11:30 am. (Subject to change)

To be a part of this incredible experience, the entry fee is N$350. Remember, safety always comes first, so feel free to bring your own support team to guide you through the swim. By implementing this challenge swim, Phillip Seidler and Daniel Seidler aim to foster the growth of open water swimming and sports in Namibia and create an unforgettable event that inspires people of all skill levels to participate and enjoy the thrill of open water swimming.

Get ready to swim, conquer, and celebrate at Teamflippy’s Mole to Mall Swim Challenge – where every stroke brings you closer to victory! See you there!
