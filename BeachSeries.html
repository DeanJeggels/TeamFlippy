Teamflippy's Beach Series

Welcome to the Teamflippy Beach Series!

Coastal community, get ready to dive into the ultimate sports adventure on Swakopmund's stunning beach, the Mole! The Teamflippy Beach Series is more than just a competition; it's a celebration of fitness, fun, and community.

Why Join the Beach Series?

  • Exciting Monthly Events: Experience the thrill of a 2km, 1km, 500-meter ocean or 50-meter sprint swim and a 5km or 2km beach run/walk every month. Perfect for athletes, families, friends, and pets!
  • Leagues for Everyone: Whether you're a casual participant, part of a corporate team, or representing your school, there's a league for you! Compete for points, win awards, and aim for the overall trophy.
  • Community Spirit: Connect with fellow Namibians and enjoy the positive energy of fitness and friendship by the ocean.
  • Beautiful Setting: Soak in the breathtaking views of Swakopmund's beaches as you challenge yourself to new heights.

Available exclusively in the Public League, Team League, and Pet-Friendly League:

  • Public League: Perfect for individuals and families looking to enjoy some friendly competition.
  • Team League: Gather your squad and compete together for ultimate team glory!
  • Pet-Friendly League: Bring your furry friends along for a fun, inclusive experience.

We also offer different categories to suit everyone:

  • Master Category: Tailored for experienced participants ready to take on the challenge.
  • Senior Category: For adult competitors who want to showcase their skills.
  • Junior Category: Ideal for younger participants eager to have fun and compete.
