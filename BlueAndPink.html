Make Your Mark at the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Blue and Pink Run!

Get ready for a fun and inspiring event! The Radio Electronic & Deopharma 5km Ladies Pink and Men’s Blue Run is designed to promote healthy living, encourage community participation, and raise awareness for gender wellness.

On May 17th, we invite you to be part of a unique event that celebrates both fitness and unity. Participants will represent their gender by wearing pink for ladies and blue for men, creating a sea of colour as we run together in support of a great cause.

Why You Should Join:

  • This event is open to runners of all levels and ages. Whether you're here for the fitness challenge, to support gender wellness, or just to be part of the community, this is an event you won’t want to miss.
  • Join us in promoting physical fitness, community spirit, and inclusivity. Sign up today and take part in an event that brings people together for a healthier, happier future.
  • As a special bonus, every participant will receive free merchandise to take home as a thank you for supporting this incredible event.
