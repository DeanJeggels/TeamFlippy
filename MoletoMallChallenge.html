Teamflippy Mole to Mall Challenge: Namibia’s Toughest Open Water Adventure!

Mole to Mall Challenge

Are you ready to push your limits and conquer Namibia’s ultimate open water challenge? The Mole to Mall Challenge is calling all endurance enthusiasts to dive into the Atlantic Ocean this Easter Sunday, 20th April 2025, for a test of strength, stamina, and determination.

Two Epic Courses:

  • Mole to Mall (4.5km): Swim the iconic route from the Mole to Platz am Meer Mall.
  • Tiger Reef to Mall (6km): Take on the longer, more demanding journey for the ultimate test.

Why Take the Challenge?

  • Experience the thrill of swimming in Namibia’s pristine coastal waters.
  • Compete with some of the best swimmers or just challenge yourself for fun.
  • Soak in the incredible scenery and celebrate your accomplishment at the finish line.

How to Join:

  • Register online at teamflippy.com.
  • Limited spots available—sign up early to secure your place!
