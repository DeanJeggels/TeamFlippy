Teamflippy's Neptune Splash: Celebrate Independence Weekend with a Splash!

Teamflippy Neptune Splash

Join us on Friday, 21st March 2025, at the stunning Mole in Swakopmund for the Teamflippy Neptune Splash, an open water swimming event like no other! Perfect for swimmers of all levels, this exciting event offers a chance to swim in the Atlantic and challenge yourself in a safe, fun, and festive environment.

Why Join the Neptune Splash?

  • Swim in the iconic Mole, surrounded by Swakopmund’s natural beauty.
  • Enjoy a vibrant community atmosphere with swimmers, spectators, and supporters.
  • Compete for personal achievement or just for the fun of it—this event is for everyone!

How to Enter:

  • Register online now at teamflippy.com.
  • Limited spots available, secure your place today!
