Join the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Lagoon Dream Run!

A Day of Fun, Fitness, and Free Merchandise

Get ready for an unforgettable experience at the Radio Electronic & Deopharma Lagoon Dream Run! This event is all about promoting healthy living, fostering community engagement, and celebrating the beauty of Walvis Bay.

On June 14th, lace up your running shoes and join us at the stunning Lagoon in Walvis Bay for a run that promises fun, fitness, and even free merchandise for all participants. Whether you're running for wellness or simply enjoying the views, this event offers something for everyone.

Why the Lagoon?

The Lagoon in Walvis Bay is the perfect setting for a run. With its tranquil waters, picturesque landscapes, and refreshing coastal breeze, it provides an ideal environment for runners of all levels. The unique combination of scenic views, wildlife, and the peaceful atmosphere makes this one of the most amazing places to run in Namibia. Imagine the beauty of the lagoon stretching out before you as you run, creating a truly inspiring experience.
